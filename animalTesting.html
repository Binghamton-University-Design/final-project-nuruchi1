Animal Testing:

Like other beauty industries, the nail polish industry has a long history of testing on animals
The testing done on these animals varies but all testing is inhumane at this point, due to the amount of data collected over the years. Some of the tests done on the animals are dripping substances into their eyes, smearing products onto their shaved or scraped skin, or forcing them to ingest or inhale huge quantities of chemicals.

As times are changing there was a wave of demand for cruelty-free products, from skin care to nail polish. There is now several companies that have changed to cruelty free and other brands that still test on animals.
