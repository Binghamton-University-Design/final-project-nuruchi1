Medias infulence on nail trends:

Starting in the late 2000's regular and social media has greatly influence nail trends.

Nail art designs were more easily acessible since social media platorms, such as Twitter, allowed anyone to see what celebrities were wearing and then replicate it. #ManicureMonday hashtag on twitter showed people doing intricate nail art at home. Nails were making statements on catwalks and in pop culture. Even now, nail art trends boom on Instagram, Pintrest and other platforms. Nail polish companies have seen the benefit to using social media to promote their new line of polish. This supports how it is easier now than ever for anyone to follow the trends and replicate designs thanks to youtube tutorials, nail art accounts on Instagram, Twitch and other social media. Beauty magazines and companies that share the trends such as Cosmopolitan have even shared tips and tricks on Snapchat, the social media app has its own user submited stories for 'nail checks'- where users can share their manicures.
