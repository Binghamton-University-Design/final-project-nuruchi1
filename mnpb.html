Male celebrity nail polish brands:

Within the past couple of years there has been a sudden rise of celebrity owned beauty brands

Most notiably four male celebrities have created their own 'inclusive' nail polish brand


Crete by Lil Yachty

Crete launched on May 21, 2021. It was marketed as "4 all genders. For you, not them!" Despite Yachty leaving Crete, the company is still selling their 10-free nail polish pens. You can purchase Crete Here


Pleasing by Harry Styles

Pleasing launched on November 29, 2021. It was marketed as an inclusive brand and has seen success since its launch. Pleasing's nailpolish is 12-free, and inclues skin care items. You can purchase Pleasing Here


UNDN LAQR by Machine Gun Kelly

UN/DN launched on December 2, 2021. It is marketed as "Not your typical nail polish", with names such as "Depessionist". UN/DN's polishes are vegan, 9-free and cruelty free. You can purchase UN/DN LAQR Here


GOLF le FLEUR by Tyler, the Creator

GOLF le FLEUR launched on December 13, 2021. It was marketed as an inclusive 13 free, non-toxic and cruelty-free polish. Tyler, the Creator has several other subsections of le FLEUR, such as purfume and acessories. Currently all three nail polishes are sold out, but you can purchase GOLF le FLEUR items Here
